If there's one thing you should know about me, it's that I love cats. Dogs can't even compare to the goofy and lovable nature of these creatures. Just take a look at this video of Cute Cats and you will know why I say this. I have had cats my whole life and plan on being a crazy cat lady when I grow up, so I have always made an effort to convert all my friends and family to cat-lovers. I have 2 of my own, named Fonzi and Elvis. My family got them both when they were kittens, but they are now 7 and 8 years old.

Fonzi

Fonzi is a gentle giant. We adopted him from a cat hoarding house, so he was very shy and anxious when we first met him. However, he broke out of his shell over time and is now a very adventurous outdoor cat. Fonzi LOVES food, naps, and discovering new trees and bushes to hide in around our neighborhood.

The Brothers

Although they are not related by blood, these boys have spent year together and have become best buds! They love to take naps, groom eachother, and steal eachothers food ;)

Elvis

Elvis is the little daredevil of the family. He has been EXTREMELY outgoing since day 1, and started exploring the outdoors as soon as we got him. But, for as much energy he has, he is a sweet guy who loves to cuddle and play with humans. Elvis' favorite activities are chasing squirrels, nuzzling against people's legs, and eating as many treats as he can.
